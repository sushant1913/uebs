Our Team

United English Boarding School is a Manahari's first english boarding school and our staff body reflects this. They are committed to delivering the best possible educational experience for every child at our school, with their own continued professional development being a priority in their career and in the school generally. Support staff are also chosen for their dedication, hard work, experience and qualifications, whether this is to directly support the pupils in the classroom or as part of the general operations team behind the educational practice. All staff are a valued part of the our School family formed by our pupils, their families, staff and external partners.
